# Element: capabilities

The infrastructure proof — warehouse, fleet, MRP, AMP, software. Why
Abel can actually deliver what the prospect needs, at their volume,
without missing a beat.

## Required content

A "What Abel runs" block. Pick 4–6 of these (don't dump all at once):

- **Gainesville HQ + warehouse** — square footage placeholder
  `[XX,XXX sq ft]` with HUMAN_REVIEW comment; staging capacity for
  multi-tier loads.
- **Door slab line** — coming online; takes lead time from 3 weeks to
  5 days. Pillar 5 in action.
- **Delivery fleet** — DFW-radius coverage, white-glove unloading, BTR
  staging. `[N]` trucks, `[X]` daily routes — placeholders with
  HUMAN_REVIEW.
- **MRP module** — material requirements planning live in Abel OS.
  Demand-forecasted PO generation; per-build kitting.
- **AMP (Abel Material Partners)** — Boise Cascade primary, with
  active negotiations on credit + lead time. Multi-vendor optionality
  via the 6-manufacturer ladder.
- **Abel OS / app.abellumber.com** — 58 models, 453 API routes, live
  since April 2026. Builders see order status, delivery ETA, invoice
  history. Not a marketing platform — operational fabric.
- **Customer portal** — quote review, PO acknowledgement, delivery
  scheduling, invoice history.
- **Audit + traceability** — every door SKU traced from manufacturer
  PO through the warehouse to the jobsite, in Abel OS.

  one-sentence description, one number.
